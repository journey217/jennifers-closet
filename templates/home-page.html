Jennifer's Closet

About Us

Jennifer's Closet is a volunteer project of Bethany Presbyterian Church established by a neighborhood mom, named Jennifer, in 2023. We offer gently used clothing in sizes infant to 3X, free of charge for Greece families in need. We are located in the church at 3000 Dewey Ave. (just north of Stone Rd.)
We do not serve thrifters or resellers.


Appointments

We offer appointments Mondays and Thursdays from 9:30 a.m. to 12 p.m. Please submit your name, email, and phone number to request additional information or book an appointment.

Donations

To donate clothing, please submit your name, email, and phone number. You will then be contacted to schedule your drop off.
We ask that all donations are not stained, damaged, or worn-out clothing.
We accept the following household items: new towels, sheet sets and comforters.
If you would like to make a financial contribution to support our project, please make the checks payable to Bethany Presbyterian Church with "Clothing Closet" on the memo, 3000 Dewey Ave., Rochester, NY 14616.



Volunteering

If you are looking to help out in the clothing closet, we are looking for people to help sort donations, replenish clothing racks, and more.
Please submit your name, email, and phone number. You will then be contacted to find a time to volunteer.
