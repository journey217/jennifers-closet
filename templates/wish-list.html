Donations

To donate clothing, please email us your name and phone number. You will then be contacted to schedule your drop off. We ask that all donations are not stained, damaged, or worn-out clothing.
If you would like to make a financial contribution to support our project, please make the checks payable to Bethany Presbyterian Church with "Clothing Closet" on the memo.
We do not accept household items.
